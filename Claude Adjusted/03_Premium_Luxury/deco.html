◆ ABOUT THE GALA ◆

Step back into the most dazzling decade of the twentieth century. THE GILDED GALA transports you to an era of unparalleled elegance, where jazz fills the air, champagne flows freely, and the spirit of the Roaring Twenties comes alive once more.

Don your finest Art Deco attire—think beaded flapper dresses, tailored tuxedos, feathered headpieces, and jeweled accessories. This New Year's Eve, we celebrate in the grandest style of 1926.

◆ THE EXPERIENCE ◆

  • Live Jazz Orchestra — The Metropolitan Ensemble performs hits of the era
  • Charleston Dance Lessons — Learn the moves that defined a generation
  • Champagne Tower — A spectacular centerpiece for midnight
  • Speakeasy Cocktails — Period-accurate libations from master mixologists
  • Photo Studio — Professional portraits in authentic 1920s settings
  • Midnight Countdown — Ring in 2027 with old-world glamour
