Brewer's Notes:

This is not beer as you know it. Project: Nebula is the seventh iteration in our attempt to deconstruct the Hazy IPA. We pushed the Phantasm powder and Nelson hops to their absolute limit.

The result is... something new. We're opening up the lab for one night to let 20 people sample Batch 007 and help us decide: is this the future, or did we go too far?
