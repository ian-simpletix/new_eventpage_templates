About the Festival

Join us for our annual celebration of Southwest culture, heritage, and community. Experience authentic regional cuisine, handmade crafts from local artisans, live mariachi music, and traditional dance performances under the desert sky.

This family-friendly festival brings together our community to honor our roots and share the warmth of our culture with visitors from near and far.

What to Expect

  • Authentic Southwest cuisine from 12+ food vendors
  • Live music featuring local mariachi and folk bands
  • Handmade crafts marketplace with 40+ artisans
  • Kids' activities including pottery painting & story time
