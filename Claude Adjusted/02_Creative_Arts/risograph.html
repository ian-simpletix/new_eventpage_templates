ABOUT THE FESTIVAL

The Print Culture Festival celebrates the tactile beauty of physical media in our digital age. Join us for a day dedicated to the art of printing, self-publishing, and independent creation.

Explore risograph printing demonstrations, browse 50+ vendor tables featuring zines and art prints, attend workshops on DIY publishing, and connect with makers from across the region.

Whether you're a seasoned printer or just discovering zine culture, this festival offers something for everyone who believes in the power of putting ink on paper.

FESTIVAL HIGHLIGHTS

  • Live Risograph Printing Demos — Watch master printers in action
  • 50+ Independent Vendors — Discover unique zines, prints & books
  • DIY Publishing Workshops — Learn the craft from the pros
  • Zine Library — Browse hundreds of self-published works
  • Letterpress Station — Create your own print to take home
  • Live Screen Printing — Watch posters come to life
