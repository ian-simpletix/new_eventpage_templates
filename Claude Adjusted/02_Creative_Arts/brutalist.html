ABOUT

This is not a concert. This is not a performance. This is an experiment in sound, space, and human tolerance.

Three acts. No comfort. No compromise. Pure sonic brutalism stripped of all pretense. We reject polish. We reject perfection. We embrace the raw, the harsh, the unfiltered.

This event is designed to challenge your expectations of what music can be. If you're looking for entertainment, go elsewhere. If you're seeking an experience that will fundamentally alter your relationship with sound, you're in the right place.
