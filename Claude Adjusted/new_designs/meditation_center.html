About the Series

Learn the fundamentals of meditation in this welcoming 6-week series designed for beginners and those returning to practice. Each session combines guided instruction, group meditation, and time for questions and reflection.

You'll explore various meditation techniques, understand the science behind mindfulness, and develop a sustainable personal practice that fits your life.

Weekly Topics

  • Week 1: Foundations of Meditation & Breath Awareness
  • Week 2: Body Scan & Present Moment Awareness
  • Week 3: Working with Thoughts & Emotions
  • Week 4: Loving-Kindness Meditation
  • Week 5: Mindfulness in Daily Life
  • Week 6: Building Your Personal Practice

What You'll Gain

  • Practical meditation techniques you can use anywhere
  • Greater awareness and emotional regulation
  • Reduced stress and improved focus
  • A supportive community of fellow practitioners
  • Tools for cultivating compassion and resilience
  • Weekly guided meditations to practice at home

What to Bring

All meditation cushions and props are provided. Just bring yourself and an open mind. Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to sit easily. A journal is optional but recommended for noting insights and reflections.
