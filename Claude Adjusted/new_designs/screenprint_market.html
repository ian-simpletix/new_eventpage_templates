ABOUT THE MARKET:

A celebration of DIY print culture featuring 40+ independent artists, printers, and zine makers selling their handmade screenprinted work. Browse limited edition posters, art prints, apparel, patches, tote bags, and more.

All work is original, hand-pulled, and made with care. No mass-produced stuff. Just real humans making cool things.

WHAT YOU'LL FIND:

  • Original screenprinted art prints & posters
  • Hand-printed t-shirts, tote bags, patches
  • Limited edition zines and artist books
  • Vintage printing equipment & supplies
  • Live printing demonstrations all day
  • DIY printing workshops (sign up at info booth)

FEATURED VENDORS:

  • GHOST PRINTS // Gig posters & music art
  • WILD TYPE PRESS // Nature-inspired prints
  • MESSY HANDS STUDIO // Colorful abstract prints
