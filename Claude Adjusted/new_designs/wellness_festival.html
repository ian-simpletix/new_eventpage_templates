About the Festival

Join us for a full day celebrating holistic health, community connection, and sustainable living. The Harvest Wellness Festival brings together yoga practitioners, local wellness vendors, organic food makers, and healing arts professionals for an inspiring outdoor experience.

Whether you're looking to try something new, deepen your wellness practice, or simply enjoy a beautiful day outdoors with like-minded people, this festival has something for everyone.

Festival Features

  • 30+ wellness workshops & movement classes throughout the day
  • Live acoustic music on two stages
  • Organic food vendors & juice bar
  • Local artisan & wellness vendor marketplace
  • Healing arts pavilion (massage, acupuncture, energy work)
  • Kids' activities & family yoga sessions
  • Sustainability showcase & community garden tours
