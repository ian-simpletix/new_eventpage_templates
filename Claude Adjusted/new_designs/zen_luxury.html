About the Experience

Experience the refined art of kaiseki, traditional Japanese haute cuisine, in an intimate setting designed for contemplation and presence. Chef Yuki Tanaka, trained in Kyoto's most respected kitchens, creates a seasonal nine-course menu honoring centuries-old techniques.

Each dish reflects the principle of ichi-go ichi-e: "one time, one meeting" — the understanding that each encounter is unique and unrepeatable.

The Menu

Our winter menu features:

  • Sakizuke — seasonal amuse-bouche to awaken the palate
  • Suimono — clear soup with winter vegetables
  • Otsukuri — sashimi selection from Tsukiji market
  • Yakimono — grilled seasonal fish with yuzu
  • Nimono — slow-simmered winter vegetables
  • Agemono — tempura course
  • Gohan — seasonal rice with accompaniments
  • Tome-wan — final miso soup
  • Mizumono — seasonal dessert

Menu varies with seasonal availability. Dietary restrictions accommodated with advance notice.
