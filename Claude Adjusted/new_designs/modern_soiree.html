Join Us for an Evening

Gather with fellow design enthusiasts, makers, and thoughtful people for an intimate evening at a private estate nestled in the Napa Valley hills. This carefully curated gathering combines beautiful surroundings, seasonal food, meaningful conversation, and live music.

In a world of constant noise, we're creating space for presence, beauty, and genuine connection. No agenda, no sponsors — just good people, good food, and good company.

The Evening

  • Welcome drinks on the garden terrace
  • Four-course farm-to-table dinner by Chef Amanda Torres
  • Wine pairings from local vintners
  • Live acoustic performance by Bay Area musicians
  • Open gardens for strolling and stargazing
  • Cozy fireside lounge with coffee and dessert

Who Should Come

This gathering is designed for curious, creative people who value quality over quantity and depth over surface. Whether you're an artist, entrepreneur, designer, or simply someone who appreciates beauty and good conversation, you'll feel at home here.

We keep the guest list intentionally small to encourage real connections. Come solo or bring a friend who'd appreciate the vibe.

Details

Dress: Garden casual — comfortable but considered. Think linen, soft fabrics, layers for the evening breeze.

Transportation: The estate is 90 minutes north of San Francisco. Carpooling encouraged — we'll connect guests for shared rides. Limited on-site parking available.

Accessibility: The venue is mostly accessible, with some outdoor terrain. Please reach out with questions or specific needs.
