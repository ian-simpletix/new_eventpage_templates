About the Retreat

Escape to the mountains for three days of rejuvenation, mindful movement, and deep relaxation. Our Mountain Yoga Retreat offers a perfect blend of daily yoga practices, guided meditation, nourishing meals, and time to reconnect with yourself and nature.

Whether you're new to yoga or have an established practice, this retreat is designed to meet you where you are and support your journey toward greater wellbeing.

  • Nature walks and free time for relaxation
