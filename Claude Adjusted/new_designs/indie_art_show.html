ABOUT THE SHOW:

Emerging Voices celebrates the work of 12 independent artists pushing boundaries in painting, printmaking, sculpture, and mixed media. This group exhibition features artists working outside traditional gallery systems, creating bold, personal, and uncompromising work.

Opening night includes artist talks, live music, and light refreshments. All work is available for purchase directly from the artists.

FEATURED ARTISTS:

  • LIA MARTINEZ - Abstract expressionist paintings
  • KAI WILSON - Carved wood sculptures
  • SOFIA NGUYEN - Risograph print series
  • MARCUS BROWN - Mixed media collages
  • ELENA GARCIA - Ceramic installations
  • + 7 more incredible artists
