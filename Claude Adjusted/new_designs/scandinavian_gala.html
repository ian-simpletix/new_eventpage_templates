About the Gala

An elegant evening celebrating the intersection of Scandinavian design, contemporary art, and community. Join us for an unforgettable night of curated exhibitions, live music, and meaningful connections.

This annual gala brings together design enthusiasts, collectors, and makers in a thoughtfully crafted environment that reflects Nordic principles of simplicity, functionality, and beauty.

Evening Program

  • Welcome reception with Scandinavian-inspired cocktails
  • Guided tour of featured design installations
  • Three-course Nordic-influenced dinner
  • Live performance by acoustic string quartet
  • Silent auction featuring works by emerging designers
  • After-hours lounge with DJ and craft cocktails

Featured Exhibitors

This year's gala showcases the work of five celebrated Scandinavian designers working across furniture, textiles, ceramics, and lighting. Each piece reflects the Nordic design ethos of form following function, with an emphasis on sustainable materials and timeless aesthetics.
