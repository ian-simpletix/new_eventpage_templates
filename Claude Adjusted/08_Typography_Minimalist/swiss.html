ABOUT THE
CONFERENCE

For three days, leading designers, educators, and practitioners gather to examine the enduring relevance of modernist design principles in today's complex visual landscape.

Through lectures, workshops, and panel discussions, we explore how clarity, precision, and functional elegance continue to shape contemporary practice across all design disciplines.

This conference is essential for anyone committed to design that serves human needs through thoughtful, systematic approaches to visual communication.
