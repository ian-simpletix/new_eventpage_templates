In the quietude of traditional Japanese gardens, discover the ancient art of tea ceremony and the profound practice of mindfulness meditation.

This daylong retreat offers a sanctuary from modern life, inviting you to slow down, breathe deeply, and experience the beauty of presence.

Led by Master Tanaka Hiroshi, a tea ceremony practitioner with over 30 years of experience, and mindfulness teacher Yuki Nakamura, this retreat blends centuries-old traditions with contemporary wellness practices.

Program

9:00 – 10:30
Morning Meditation & Garden Walk
Begin your day with guided walking meditation through the Zen garden, followed by seated meditation in the tea house.
10:45 – 12:30
Introduction to Tea Ceremony
Learn the fundamental principles and movements of Chanoyu, the Way of Tea, including proper etiquette and the philosophy behind each gesture.
12:30 – 1:30
Mindful Vegetarian Lunch
Enjoy a traditional Shojin Ryori Buddhist vegetarian meal, practicing mindful eating in silence.
1:45 – 3:30
Tea Ceremony Practice
Participate in your own tea ceremony, preparing and serving matcha to fellow participants under Master Tanaka's guidance.
3:45 – 5:00
Closing Meditation & Reflection
Integrate the day's experiences through guided meditation and group reflection, taking home practices for daily life.
