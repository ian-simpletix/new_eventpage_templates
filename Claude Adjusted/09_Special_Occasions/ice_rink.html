An Evening of Elegance and Grace

February 14, 2026

A Night to Remember

Experience the artistry and athleticism of world-class figure skating as internationally acclaimed performers take to the ice for an unforgettable evening of beauty, grace, and emotion.

This exclusive gala performance brings together Olympic medalists, world champions, and rising stars in an elegant showcase that celebrates the timeless art of figure skating.

Featured Performers

Elena Volkov

Olympic Gold Medalist

Two-time Olympic champion renowned for her technical precision and artistic expression. Known for breathtaking spins and elegant choreography.

Marcus Chen

World Champion

Three-time world champion celebrated for powerful jumps and innovative programs. A master of combining athleticism with artistry.

Isabelle Dubois & Thomas Laurent

Pairs Champions

European champions known for their seamless synchronization and romantic performances. A dynamic duo that captivates audiences worldwide.

Yuki Tanaka

Rising Star

Junior world champion and rising sensation. Watch this young talent showcase the future of figure skating with fresh, innovative routines.

Evening Program

Act I: Classical Elegance

Elena Volkov

A stunning interpretation of Tchaikovsky's Swan Lake, featuring breathtaking spirals and elegant footwork that showcases classical skating at its finest.

Act II: Power and Precision

Marcus Chen

An athletic showcase featuring quadruple jumps and dynamic choreography set to contemporary music, demonstrating the perfect marriage of technical skill and artistry.

Act III: Romance on Ice

Isabelle Dubois & Thomas Laurent

A romantic pairs performance featuring intricate lifts, throws, and synchronized elements that tell a story of love and partnership.

Act IV: The Future of Skating

Yuki Tanaka

An innovative program that pushes boundaries with modern choreography and technical elements, showcasing the evolution of figure skating.

Finale: All-Star Showcase

Full Cast

All performers unite for a spectacular closing number that celebrates the artistry and athleticism of figure skating, leaving audiences inspired.
