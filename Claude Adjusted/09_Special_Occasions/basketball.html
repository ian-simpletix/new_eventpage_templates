What's Included

Registration includes everything your team needs for an amazing tournament experience!

  • Minimum 3 guaranteed games per team
  • Professional referees and scorekeepers
  • Championship trophies for 1st, 2nd, 3rd place teams
  • Medals for all championship team players
  • Participation awards for all players
  • Digital tournament bracket and live scoring
  • Team photos and action photography
  • Food vendors and concessions on site
  • Free parking for all participants and families

We want your family to have a great experience! The City Sports Complex offers comfortable seating with great views of all courts. Food trucks and vendors will be on site all weekend. Free Wi-Fi available throughout the facility.
