Welcome to Winter Wonderland

Step into a magical winter celebration featuring artisan vendors, seasonal treats, live music, and activities for the whole family. Experience the warmth and joy of the season in a festive, welcoming atmosphere.

Our Winter Wonderland Market brings together local artisans, performers, and food vendors to create an unforgettable holiday experience that celebrates community and the beauty of winter.

Good to Know

Dress Warmly: The market is primarily outdoors with some covered areas. We recommend dressing in layers and wearing comfortable winter clothing.

Accessibility: The venue is wheelchair accessible. Heated rest areas are available throughout the market. Please contact us for any special accommodations.

Parking: Free parking available in the downtown garage. Shuttle service runs continuously from remote lots.

What's Included: Admission includes access to all entertainment, activities, and the market. Food, beverages, ice skating rental, and vendor purchases are separate.
