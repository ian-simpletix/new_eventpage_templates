About This Gathering

Hear ye, hear ye! Be cordially invited to attend the grandest Medieval Faire this side of the kingdom. Witness jousting tournaments, partake in feasting most bountiful, and enjoy performances by minstrels, jesters, and fire dancers.

Don thy finest medieval garb and join us for a day of merriment, revelry, and adventure worthy of song and legend.

Entertainments & Diversions

  • Jousting Tournament (High Noon & Eventide)
  • Archery Contest for All Ages
  • Medieval Marketplace with Artisans & Craftsmen
  • Minstrel Performances Throughout the Day
  • Feast Hall Serving Period-Authentic Fare
