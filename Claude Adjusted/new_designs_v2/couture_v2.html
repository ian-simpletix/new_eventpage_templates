You are cordially invited to the tenth annual Winter Ball, benefiting the Museum's costume and textile arts fund. Join us for an unforgettable evening of champagne, orchestral music, and fine art.

This year's gala celebrates the intersection of contemporary fashion and classical form, featuring a curated exhibition of haute couture pieces alongside performances from the Metropolitan Chamber Orchestra.

The Evening

  • Champagne reception with passed hors d'oeuvres
  • Private exhibition viewing
  • Three-course seated dinner
  • Live orchestral performance
  • After-party with DJ in the sculpture garden

Dress Code

Strictly Black Tie. Avant-Garde interpretation is encouraged. The evening's theme is "Modern Elegance." We invite guests to embrace bold silhouettes and unexpected textures while maintaining formal sophistication.
