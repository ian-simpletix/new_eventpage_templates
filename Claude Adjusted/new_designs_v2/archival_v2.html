I. ABSTRACT

A multi-sensory performance exploring the nature of truth in post-digital society. Artist collective "The Institute" presents a 90-minute immersive work combining live performance, archival footage, and audience participation.

This experimental work questions the boundaries between documentation and fiction, observer and observed, truth and performance.

II. EVIDENCE

  • Live performance in Black Box theater
  • Archival video projections
  • Interactive audience components
  • Post-performance discussion
  • Limited to 50 participants per session

III. PROTOCOL

Participants are asked to arrive 15 minutes prior to start time. No late entry permitted once performance begins. Photography and recording devices prohibited.

WARNING: This work contains strobing lights and may not be suitable for all audiences.
