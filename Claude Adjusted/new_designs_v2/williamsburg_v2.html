About The Event

A one-night-only screening of independent short films from five of NYC's most innovative new directors. Hosted at The Old Ironworks, The Midnight Projection is a celebration of lo-fi cinema.

The Films

  • "Asphalt" (Dir. J. Chen) - 15 min
  • "Subway Serenade" (Dir. M. Ramirez) - 10 min
  • "Neon & Concrete" (Dir. K. Williams) - 12 min
  • "Late Train" (Dir. S. Park) - 18 min
  • "Bodega Dreams" (Dir. L. Martinez) - 14 min

What to Expect

All five films explore themes of urban life, isolation, and connection in contemporary New York. Post-screening Q&A with the directors and refreshments will follow in the adjacent gallery space.

This is a BYOB event. Outside food and drink welcome. Cash bar available.
