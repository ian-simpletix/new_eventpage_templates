Reserve Your Place

In the quietude of traditional Japanese gardens, discover the ancient art of tea ceremony and the profound practice of mindfulness meditation.

This daylong retreat offers a sanctuary from modern life, inviting you to slow down, breathe deeply, and experience the beauty of presence.

PROGRAM

  • 9:00 – 10:30
    Morning Meditation & Garden Walk
    Begin your day with guided walking meditation through the Zen garden.
  • 10:45 – 12:30
    Introduction to Tea Ceremony
    Learn the fundamental principles of Chanoyu, the Way of Tea.
  • 12:30 – 1:30
    Mindful Vegetarian Lunch
    Traditional Shojin Ryori Buddhist vegetarian meal.
  • 1:45 – 3:30
    Tea Ceremony Practice
    Prepare and serve matcha under Master Tanaka's guidance.
  • 3:45 – 5:00
    Closing Meditation & Reflection
    Integrate the day's experiences through guided meditation.

Led by Master Tanaka Hiroshi, a tea ceremony practitioner with over 30 years of experience, and mindfulness teacher Yuki Nakamura.
