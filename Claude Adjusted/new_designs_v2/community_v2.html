What's Happening?

Get your sweet tooth ready! We're hosting our 10th annual bake sale to raise money for new playground equipment. Come grab a cookie, a cupcake, or a whole pie!

All proceeds go directly to upgrading our school playground with safe, modern equipment that all kids can enjoy.

What We're Selling

  • Homemade cookies and brownies
  • Cupcakes and cake slices
  • Whole pies (pre-order recommended!)
  • Gluten-free and vegan options available
  • Coffee and lemonade

Get Involved!

Want to donate some baked goods? We'd love your help! Contact the PTA office or drop off items Friday evening (5-7 PM) or Saturday morning before 9:30 AM.

Can't bake? No problem! We also need volunteers to help run the sale, set up tables, and clean up. Every bit helps!
