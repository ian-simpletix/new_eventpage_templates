About the Event

Join us for an enchanting spring evening in the historic gardens of Rosewood Estate. As the sun sets over the flower beds and hedgerows, enjoy champagne, seasonal canapés, and the company of fellow garden enthusiasts and nature lovers.

This intimate gathering is limited to 60 guests to preserve the tranquil atmosphere of the gardens.

The Experience

  • Champagne and botanical cocktails
  • Seasonal canapés by local farm-to-table caterer
  • Guided garden tour with the estate horticulturist
  • Live acoustic music (classical guitar and violin)
  • Flower arranging demonstration
  • Take-home botanical favor from the garden

The Gardens

Rosewood Estate features three acres of formal gardens originally designed in 1890. Spring blooms include roses, peonies, irises, and over 200 varieties of perennials. The garden is maintained using organic methods and serves as a haven for pollinators and songbirds.

Practical Details

Parking: Valet service provided. Accessibility: Garden paths are gravel—flat shoes recommended. Weather: Event held rain or shine; covered pavilion available.
