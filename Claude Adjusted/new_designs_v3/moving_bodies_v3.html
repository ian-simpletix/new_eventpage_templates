About the Run

Join us every Saturday morning for a friendly 5K run/walk through Riverside Park. All fitness levels welcome—whether you're training for a marathon or just getting started, you'll find your pace here.

We have pace groups from 8-minute miles to 15-minute miles, plus walkers. No one gets left behind!

What to Expect

  • Free event—just show up!
  • Pace groups led by experienced runners
  • Scenic 5K route through the park
  • Water stations along the route
  • Coffee and bagels after (optional $5)
  • Friendly, supportive community

What to Bring

  • Running shoes and comfortable clothes
  • Water bottle
  • Positive attitude!
