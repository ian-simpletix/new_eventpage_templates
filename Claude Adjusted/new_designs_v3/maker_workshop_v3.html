About the Workshop

Learn the fundamentals of woodworking in this beginner-friendly, full-day workshop. You'll design and build your own hardwood cutting board from scratch, learning essential techniques including measuring, cutting, gluing, sanding, and finishing.

No experience required. All tools, materials, and safety equipment provided. Take home a beautiful, functional cutting board you made with your own hands.

What You'll Learn

  • Safety protocols and proper tool handling
  • Wood selection and grain direction
  • Accurate measuring and marking
  • Table saw and jointer basics
  • Gluing techniques and clamping
  • Sanding progression and finishing
  • Food-safe wood treatments

What's Included

  • 6 hours of hands-on instruction
  • All hardwood materials (maple, walnut, cherry)
  • Use of all workshop tools and equipment
  • Safety equipment (goggles, ear protection)
  • Lunch and snacks
  • Your finished cutting board to take home
  • Digital guide with care instructions

What to Bring

  • Closed-toe shoes (required)
  • Clothes you don't mind getting dusty
  • Hair tie if you have long hair
  • Water bottle
