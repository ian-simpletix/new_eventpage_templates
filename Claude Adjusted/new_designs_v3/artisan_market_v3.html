About the Market

Every Saturday morning, our downtown plaza transforms into a vibrant gathering of local makers, farmers, food vendors, and musicians. Browse handmade goods, pick up fresh produce, grab breakfast from a food truck, and enjoy live music.

Free entry. All vendors are local artisans and small businesses within 50 miles of downtown.

What You'll Find

  • Handmade ceramics, jewelry, and textiles
  • Farm-fresh produce and flowers
  • Baked goods and artisan food products
  • Coffee, breakfast, and lunch vendors
  • Live music from local musicians
  • Kids' activities and face painting

Vendor Info

Interested in being a vendor? We welcome local makers and small food businesses. Booth fees start at $40/day. Email us at vendors@localmakerscollective.org for an application.
