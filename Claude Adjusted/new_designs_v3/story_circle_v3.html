About the Event

Join us for an intimate evening with bestselling author Carmen Torres as she reads from her acclaimed new novel, The Memory Keeper's Daughter, and discusses her writing process, inspiration, and the themes of memory, family, and belonging that run through her work.

The reading will be followed by a Q&A session and book signing. Copies of the book will be available for purchase.

About the Book

The Memory Keeper's Daughter tells the story of three generations of women navigating identity, loss, and resilience in 1970s Mexico City. Torres weaves together magical realism and historical fiction in a narrative that Kirkus Reviews calls "luminous and unforgettable."
