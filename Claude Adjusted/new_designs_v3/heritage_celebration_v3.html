About the Festival

Join us for our 35th annual Lunar New Year Festival, a joyful celebration of Chinese heritage, culture, and community. Experience traditional performances, authentic cuisine, cultural demonstrations, and activities for all ages as we welcome the Year of the Horse.

This free, family-friendly festival is open to everyone. Come celebrate with us!

Festival Highlights

  • Lion and dragon dance performances
  • Traditional music and dance showcases
  • Martial arts demonstrations
  • Authentic food vendors
  • Calligraphy and paper-cutting workshops
  • Children's craft activities
  • Cultural exhibitions and displays

Performance Schedule

  • 12:00 PM: Opening ceremony and lion dance
  • 1:30 PM: Traditional music ensemble
  • 3:00 PM: Martial arts demonstration
  • 5:00 PM: Dance performances
  • 7:00 PM: Dragon dance finale
