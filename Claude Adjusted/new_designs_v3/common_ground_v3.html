About the Event

Join us for our annual fundraising gala supporting Common Ground's mission to provide accessible education and mentorship programs to underserved youth in our community. An evening of connection, inspiration, and impact.

All proceeds directly support scholarship programs, after-school tutoring, and college preparation services for students who need them most.

The Evening Includes

  • Reception with appetizers and drinks
  • Three-course dinner
  • Keynote address from Dr. Maria Chen
  • Student testimonials and success stories
  • Silent auction
  • Live entertainment
